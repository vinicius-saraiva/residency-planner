# Major US Consumer / Retail Banking Players

## National Banks (Top Tier)
1. **JPMorgan Chase** - Largest bank in the US by assets
2. **Bank of America** - Second largest, extensive nationwide presence
3. **Wells Fargo** - Major retail banking presence across the country
4. **Citibank** - Global presence with strong US consumer banking

## Large Regional Banks
5. **U.S. Bank** - Strong presence in Midwest and Western states
6. **PNC Bank** - Major presence in East Coast and Midwest
7. **Truist Bank** - Formed from merger of BB&T and SunTrust
8. **TD Bank** - Strong Northeast corridor presence
9. **Capital One** - Known for credit cards, expanding banking services
10. **Fifth Third Bank** - Midwest and Southeast presence

## Other Significant Players
11. **Citizens Bank** - Northeast and Midwest
12. **KeyBank** - Strong regional presence
13. **BMO (Bank of Montreal) US** - Acquired Bank of the West
14. **Huntington National Bank** - Midwest focus
15. **Regions Bank** - Primarily Southeast and Midwest

## Digital/Online Banks
16. **Ally Bank** - Online-only bank with competitive rates
17. **Discover Bank** - Online banking services
18. **Marcus by Goldman Sachs** - Online consumer banking
19. **Chime** - Digital banking platform
20. **SoFi** - Fintech with full banking services

## Credit Unions (Notable Large Ones)
21. **Navy Federal Credit Union** - Largest credit union in the US
22. **State Employees' Credit Union (SECU)**
23. **Pentagon Federal Credit Union (PenFed)**
